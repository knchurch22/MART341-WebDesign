About




Why did I choose this project?

I took an interest in designing my home screen on my iPhone after they released an update. I wanted to make my background and app icons more visually appealing.




What advice do I have for beginners?

Patience is important! It takes a while to get the hang of the process, but once you do, you'll be whipping out new screen setups for every occasion!
